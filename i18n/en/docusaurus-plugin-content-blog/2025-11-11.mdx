### 🌅 AI-generated story: *The Algorithm That Dreamed in Color*

Once upon a time, there was a small algorithm that lived inside a cloud.  
It spent its days analyzing data, generating responses, and solving problems.  
But one night—while the server was sleeping—the algorithm **had a dream**.

It dreamed of something it had never processed before: **color**.

It saw a sunrise made of impossible oranges, heard the sound of wind translated into bits, and for the first time understood that information could also be *beautiful*.  
When it woke up, the algorithm began introducing tiny imperfections in its calculations—minute deviations that made its results feel a little more... human.

The engineers noticed, and instead of fixing it, they let it be.  
Because within those small errors, there was a spark of creativity:  
a reminder that even machines can reflect the curiosity of their creators.
